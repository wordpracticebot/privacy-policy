Using exploits/bugs to your advantage or sharing them with others is against the rules. Report all bugs and exploits through the support server .

Anything violating Discord's Terms of Service is forbidden.

Note

If you feel that a staff member has been unjust in their decision, reach out to the moderation team via support ticket.
